Oatmeal Cookies

Description

Healthy cookies with crunchy texture on the outside but soft on the inside.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal 230gr
  • Flour 100gr
  • Mascabo sugar 100gr
  • Sunflower oil 100ml
  • Eggs 2
  • Vanilla essence 1tsp

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with oatmeal and mascabo sugar.
  2. Add the eggs and the oil, mix again.
  3. Let the mix hydrate for about 20 min.
  4. Make balls and press them on a baking tray.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 15 min.
