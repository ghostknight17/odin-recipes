Frozen Limoncello

Description

This frozen limoncello is a refreshing and slushy cocktail, made with only 3 ingredients, and that's counting the ice!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 ounces limoncello
  • 1 ounce fresh squeezed lemon juice

Steps

  1. Place ice, Limoncello and lemon juice in the jar of a blender. Blend until ice is completely slushy.
