Pancakes

Description

Basic but versatile thin pancakes, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Milk 250ml
  • Flour 135gr
  • Eggs 2
  • Vanilla essence 1tsp

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and add the eggs an the milk, mix well.
  2. Let the mix hydrate in the fridge for about 5 min.
  3. Put a ladle of mix in a previously heated non-stick pan
  4. Let it cook for 1 min and flip it.
  5. Let the other side cook for another minute and put it on a plate.
  6. Repeat the steps 3 to 5 for each pancake.
