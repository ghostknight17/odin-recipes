Flan

Description

Classic homemade flan.

Ingredients

  • Milk 500ml
  • Sugar 100gr
  • Eggs 4/5
  • Vanilla essence 1tsp

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs with the sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and the vanilla essence and mix again.
  3. Pour it on a ramekin with caramel, put the ramekin on a baking tray with warm water and cover it with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake for 40 to 60 min at 160°.
  5. Take the ramekin out of the tray and let it cool to room temperature, then place it on the fridge covered with foil for about 6 hours.
  6. To serve run a knife around the borders of the ramekin, then quickly and gently flip the ramekin over a plate.
